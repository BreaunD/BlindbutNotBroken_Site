Victory Over Blindness Ministries

A faith-based outreach of Blind But Not Broken, offering prayer, encouragement, and Christian community for blind, visually impaired, and disabled believers — because faith and independence walk the same road.

Our Ministry

"Walk By faith, Not by Sight" 2 Cor. 5-7.

Victory Over Blindness Ministries exists to remind blind, visually impaired, and disabled believers that their condition is not a barrier to purpose, calling, or relationship with God. We offer spiritual encouragement alongside the practical support Blind But Not Broken provides — because independence and faith grow together, not apart.

Rooted in Scripture and grounded in real community, our ministry walks alongside individuals navigating vision loss or disability who are seeking hope, connection, and a faith community that understands their journey.

What We Offer

Spiritual support, built for real life.

Prayer & Encouragement

One-on-one and group prayer support for individuals and families navigating blindness, vision loss, or disability.

Faith Community

A welcoming space to connect with other believers who understand the lived experience of disability — no one has to walk this alone.

Accessible Scripture & Resources

Guidance toward Braille, audio, and large-print Scripture and devotional resources for blind and low-vision believers.

Ministry Outreach

Partnering with churches and faith communities to grow understanding, inclusion, and accessibility for disabled congregants.

Ministry Leadership

Led in faith, walking the same path.

Suley Wilson

Suley Wilson is an ordained minister and the founder of Victory Over Blindness Ministries. Speaking from personal experience navigating blindness, Suley leads this ministry with the conviction that faith and independence are not opposites — they walk the same road. Suley also serves as Chief Executive Officer of Blind But Not Broken.
