Why Your Donation Matters

Your generosity directly supports efforts to:

Raise awareness

Increase awareness of blindness, deafness, and disability-related challenges across our communities.

Promote inclusion

Advance accessibility and inclusion so more people can fully participate in civic, economic, and social life.

Strengthen outreach

Grow community outreach programs that connect individuals with vital resources.

Support independent living

Back housing and independent-living initiatives that help people live with dignity and independence.
